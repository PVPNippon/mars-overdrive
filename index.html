MARS OVERDRIVE

Fulfill your destiny as the ultimate space-age hero

For centuries, we’ve looked to the stars, dreaming of life beyond Earth. Now, the future of Mars colonization is in your hands.
A spaceship hurtles toward Mars, and you’re the only one keeping it stocked. Tap to send the right supplies, dodge dangerous cargo, and keep the mission on track. One mistake could mean disaster.
Not everything is what it seems. Some supplies are essential, while others could doom the mission. Think fast, memorize key items, and make split-second calls before it’s too late.
The closer the ship gets to Mars, the faster things move. Can you keep up, or will the dream of Mars slip through humanity's fingers?
